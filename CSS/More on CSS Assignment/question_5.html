Google Fonts

What is Google Fonts?

Google Fonts, also known as Google Web Fonts, is a library of over a thousand open source font families. It has a collective web directory for scanning libraries and APIs for accessing fonts via CSS and Android. You can get it from the website in over 135 languages.The Google Fonts Catalog facilitates font design, exploration, and use of the service with over 48 trillion total font views.

Why do we use Google Fonts?

Google Fonts makes it easy to bring personality and performance to your websites and products. Google's robust catalog of open-source fonts and icons, makes it easy to integrate expressive type and icons seamlessly窶馬o matter where you are in the world.
